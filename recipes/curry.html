A simple, yet delicious meal any one can make at home. With only a few ingredients and some basic prep. Anyone can make this right now!

Ingredients

Here are the ingredients you will need. These ingredients can easily be catered to your taste, so add more or less of what you like!

  • 2 Onions
  • 2 Carrots
  • 3 Potatoes
  • 2 Garlic Cloves
  • 1 tbsp Butter
  • 5 cups Water or Stock
  • Beef or protein of your choice
  • 8 oz of Japanese Cury Roux (Golden Curry, Java Curry, etc.)

Special Ingredients

These ingredients are optional, however they do enhance the dish with some sweetness!

  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Ketchup
  • 1 tbsp Honey
  • 1 Red Apple

Steps

Steps do not include prepping or making the rice, so make sure to do that before or while making the dish.

  1. Prep ingredients
    • Peel & cut potatoes into chunks
    • Soak potatoes in water for 15 minutes
    • Peel & cut carrots into chunks
    • Slice Onions (1/2 inch wide)
    • Mine Garlic
    • Pat beef dry & salt
    • Peel & grate Apples (optional)
  2. Pre-heat pot with oil over medium heat
    • Once heated, lightly sear beef for 2 minutes
    • Remove beef chunks from heat
  3. Add butter to the pot and melt for a few seconds
    • Saute Onions until Caramelized for 8 minutes
    • Add garlic & saute for 30 seconds
    • Add and mix in carrots & potatotes for 30 to 60 seconds
  4. Add beef back and 5 cups of water or stock
  5. (Optional) Add special ingredients
  6. Cover pot with lid and simmer for 20 minutes
  7. Once cooked add curry roux
  8. Simmer over medium-low heat for 15 minutes
